# erudite
## Start pages are probably little more than empty.

Do you often search queries in some __specific websites__, or are you using __multiple search engines__?
### __Erudite has a search bar that can switch to your preferred target search engine or website by scrolling on.__
***
Do you ever question the usefulness of the pretty bookmark cards for the sites you ever remember and frequently visit?
### Erudite algorithmically display your notes every time you open a tab, so you will not forget. 
***
Do the static backgrounds bore you?
### Erudite displays a beautiful random picture every time.
